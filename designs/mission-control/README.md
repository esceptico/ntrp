# ntrp Mission Control mockups

Standalone HTML design explorations for a coherent Mission Control layer.

## Files

- `01-home.html` — Mission Control home: attention-first operational queue.
- `02-clearance-queue.html` — approval/clearance decision surface.
- `03-objective-detail.html` — objective-native detail page with plan, authority, evidence, artifacts, workers.
- `04-safety-check.html` — away-mode/autonomy summary.

## Thesis

Mission Control is the attention and authority layer for delegated AI work.

It should answer:

> What are my agents doing, what needs me, what changed, what is risky, and what can happen without my approval?

## Design stance

- Objectives first, agents second.
- Attention is the product.
- Authority boundaries are visible everywhere.
- Evidence beats raw logs.
- Intervention must always be obvious.
- No fake precision.

These are static mockups only; no external assets or runtime dependencies.
