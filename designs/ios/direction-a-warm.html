How do I dial my 20V supply down to 5V for the driver stage?

A few options, cheapest to cleanest:

  • Buck converter — best $2 you'll spend; set it to 5V and you're done.
  • Resistor divider — fine for a reference, useless under load.
  • Linear regulator — a 7805 works but wastes the 15V drop as heat.
search LM2596 buck module 20V → 5V

For your driver, go with the buck converter — it stays cool and holds 5V even when the gate draws current.
